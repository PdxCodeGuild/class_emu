What is this Buff Bird Blog about?
Buff Bird Blog is THE website to come for bodybuilding birds and fans of feathered creatures with amazing arms. Our home page pays homage to this the hulkest hunks the birdbuilder community has to offer. Our site also offers references on both birds and bodybuilding for anyone interested in joining the birdbuilding community.
